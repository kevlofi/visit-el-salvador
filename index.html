Visit El Salvador

Experience Central America's best-kept secret: where ancient Mayan ruins meet pristine beaches, vibrant culture, and world-class surf.

Three activities to do in El Salvador

National Palace

Tour this stunning San Salvador palace, where Neo-Gothic, Neoclassical, and Renaissance designs merge.

Tazumal
Archaeological Site

Explore this ancient Maya site featuring a climbable pyramid with panoramic views. Visit the museum afterward to see Mayan pottery and stone sculptures.

Lake Coatepeque

Kayak or paddleboard on this pristine crater lake, where turquoise waters meet verdant hillsides.
